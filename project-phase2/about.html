why astoria gates

We love Astoria is a website focused on the great community of Astoria, Queens. Through our posts, pictures, events and commentary, we try to spread our love and knowledge of Astoria to you. Whether you’ve just moved to Astoria or have lived here all your life, we hope you’ll find this website useful and informative—and that you’ll tell others about it. We dedicate this website to you. Always be open with us, let us know what you’re thinking , and we’ll do the same. The conversation won’t be dull, and it might get contrarian at times, but we can’t wait to start it.
